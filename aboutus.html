About Us

now.gg, Inc. is the largest cloud-powered mobile-gaming company, with 6B+ minutes played monthly and 20M+ MAU. It enables 1-click play of mobile games without the need to download on mobile phones (iOS & Android), PCs, Macs and Chromebooks. now.gg, Inc.'s breakthrough technology has reduced the cost of the cloud 100x, unlocking transformative forces for the mobile-gaming industry: Change in discovery surface, instant deep gameplay experiences, and decoupling of platform and payments.

With now.gg, Inc., cloud gameplay is available to gamers for free, and developers can embed gameplay anywhere - in gaming portals, discussion forums, game developer websites, PC/Mac launchers, YouTube and Twitch - fundamentally transforming the discovery surface of games.

now.gg, Inc. delivers a multi-platform service for every mobile game, enabling high margin Web-based payments and the decoupling of the gameplay platform from payments. A boost in discovery surface, the decoupling of platform and payments, and the ability to click and instantly jump to a deep experience in the game, combine to drive unlimited new business opportunities for all industry players, including developers, publishers and streamers.

now.gg, Inc. is headquartered in the Silicon Valley and its 500+ employees are distributed over 20 countries. It is backed by leading venture capital firms in the US, Japan, Korea and India.
